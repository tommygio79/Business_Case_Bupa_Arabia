Okadoc Case Study – Department of Health (DOH) Abu Dhabi

✨ Deployment Year: 2024
🏥 Industry: Healthcare
🏛️ Sector: Public
👨‍⚕️ Doctors: 4,800+
🇦🇪 Territory: UAE

🚫 Challenges

Okadoc was tasked with developing an HIS/EMR aggregator across Abu Dhabi’s healthcare ecosystem. The goal: unify a complex, fragmented system of providers and health data, while ensuring privacy, compliance, and seamless integration — all to power the Sahatna app.

🔧 Services Provided

  • Patient Experience
  • Strategy & Digital Transformation
  • IT Development & Engineering
  • Telehealth
  • Omnichannel Communication
  • Training & Onboarding
  • Change Management

🚀 Solutions Deployed

  • Patient Engagement Platform
  • Hospital Efficiency Platform
  • Digital Front Door
  • Video Consultation Platform
  • HIS/EMR Integration
  • Mobile App Development

🌟 Outcomes

The project enabled the launch of the Sahatna app at GITEX 2024, offering unified access to healthcare services, records, and genomic reports across Abu Dhabi. Over 60% of providers are connected, improving convenience, health outcomes, and provider efficiency — aligning with the emirate’s goal of a smart, patient-centric ecosystem.
